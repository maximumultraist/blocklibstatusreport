Outline

  • A single library that will take care of your blockchain appending and searching needs
  • Include Ethereum, Bitcoin, Litecoin, and others
  • Can transfer tokens and check balances
  • Provides methods that transform request to JSON
  • Establish connection to node, communicate via RPC
